California Window Tint Laws (2025): What You Need to Know

  • Front Side Windows: Must allow ≥70% of light in (Maximum 30% tint; no dark limo tints allowed).
  • Windshield: Only top 4 inches ("eyebrow") may be tinted; rest must be clear.
  • Rear & Back Side Windows: Any darkness allowed for sedans, SUVs, and vans.
  • Mirrors: Dual side mirrors required if back window is tinted.
  • Color: No red, blue, or amber tints permitted on any window.

Warning: Non-compliant tints can result in fix-it tickets and fines. Police can use a light meter to test tint darkness. All Amaze Tints installs comply with CA law—ask us if you want a legal-limit shade.
